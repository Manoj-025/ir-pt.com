Our Journey

From Idea to Impact.

Every milestone that shaped who we are today.

2022 · Q1
ir-pt Edtech Founded

Started with a simple desk in Bhopal. Our founders noticed that 12th class students had no reliable, unbiased career guidance. ir-pt Edtech was born to fill that gap.

2022 · Q2
First 50 Students Guided

Within 6 months, we guided 50+ students into the right colleges. Word spread fast — parents began recommending ir-pt Edtech to their friends and neighbours.

2023 · Q3
20 College Partnerships

Established official partnerships with 20+ Bhopal colleges. We could now secure priority seats and better deals for students — a key milestone.

2023 · Q4
Project & Thesis Support

Launched dedicated service for final year students needing help with projects, dissertations, and thesis. Served 100+ students in the first 3 months.

2024 · Q5
200+ Students · Placement Launch

Crossed the 200 students milestone and launched our placement support division — resume building, mock interviews, and corporate tie-ups.

2024 · Q6
New Office — Sagar Landmark

Moved to LG-03, Sagar Landmark Complex, Ayodhya Bypass Road — a proper consultancy space with dedicated counselling rooms and student facilities.

2025 · Now
500+ Students · 50+ Colleges · Job Finder

ir-pt Edtech has helped 500+ students, partnered with 50+ colleges, and launched a Job Finder platform — Bhopal's one-stop career solution.
